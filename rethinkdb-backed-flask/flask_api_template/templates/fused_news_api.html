Fused News API Documentation

Here you will find information on consuming the fused news api.



ADDING NEWS

POST /api/v1/fusednews - Add some new news to our database!

GETTING NEWS

GET /api/v1/fusednews - Get all the fused news from our database!
GET /api/v1/fusednews/<id> - Get a specific article from our database!

DELETING NEWS

DELETE /api/v1/fusednews/<id> - Get all the fused news from our database!
